Who we are

We are the 5th generation decendents of arrivals to Penang from Ceylon (now Sri Lanka) in the late 19th Century, a part of a larger influx who arrived at the behest of the British colonial administration to assist in the development and administration of the land.

Some worked for the Government of the day. Some were involved in the development of the country's infrastructure (transport, health, education). Still others opened up the hinterland for growing rubber, a commodity in great demand then. Our origins were in plantations and education. With each successive generation the family gradually dispersed far and wide across the Commonwealth.

At Family gatherings, talk usually gravitates towards the days of the earlier generations, and their lives and achievements in Penang.

Hickory replicates the atmosphere of a bygone era where the pace was slower and life more tranquil. It is also a focus for the diaspora to call Home.

We hope to provide You with an experience far removed from the hustle and bustle of City life; a back-to-nature experience with long walks in the woods; and an atmosphere that encourages reading and writing, in a carefree and cooler setting.

We invite you to Hickory to savour the solitude of nightfall on Penang Hill.
