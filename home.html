Welcome to Hickory

Hickory is a century-old Heritage bungalow that has been converted into a 15-room Guesthouse. It is perched on the eastern slope of Penang Hill at an elevation of 650m (2100ft). It provides panoramic views of Georgetown below, and of the mainland beyond. Sunrises from this vantage are particularly spectacular.

Hickory sits on half an acre of secluded woodland and manicured lawns. The nearest neighbours are out of sight. The surroundings are lush and green, with only the occasional sounds of birds, monkeys, squirrels and rushing water to disturb the peace.

The days are cool and the evenings cooler. A cool evening breeze rolls down the hill at the rear and through the house and out again before rolling on downhill. Temperatures at this elevation are typically 6°C (11°F) lower than at the foothills.

Get away from the pace and pressure of city life. Enjoy the tranquillity and solitude of Penang Hill.
